Here are 12 important bullet points from the Code of Conduct:

  1. Enrollment in the Program constitutes acceptance of the Code of Conduct by both students and parents/guardians, including any future updates.
  2. The code applies to all activities - on-premises, outside premises, and online (including internships, social media, competitions, and remote work).
  3. Attendance is mandatory for all classes, sessions, workshops, and events. Unapproved absences may result in suspension, expulsion, and denial of certification and placement support.
  4. Students must maintain a strict dress code (smart casual) - no revealing clothes, ripped clothing, inappropriate slogans, or casual wear like pajamas and flip-flops.
  5. Program integrity violations include cheating, plagiarism, unauthorized collaboration, sharing content/login credentials, and misrepresenting attendance.
  6. Public Display of Affection (PDA) is strictly prohibited on premises, including kissing, hugging, and other intimate physical contact.
  7. Zero tolerance for possession, consumption, distribution, or sale of drugs, alcohol, tobacco products on premises.
  8. Sexual harassment in any form is prohibited and will be dealt with according to POSH Act, with incidents to be reported to Dakshayani Yellanki.
  9. Ragging, discrimination, and harassment based on race, gender, religion, or any other characteristic are strictly prohibited.
  10. Students must wear ID cards visibly at all times and obtain prior written approval for any media activities involving NxtWave/NIAT.
  11. Disciplinary actions include warnings, suspension, monetary fines, expulsion, and withholding/cancellation of certificates.
  12. Disruptive conduct, unauthorized recording, property damage, and making false/malicious statements against NxtWave/NIAT are prohibited and subject to disciplinary action.
